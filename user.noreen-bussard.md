I have faint memories of accompanying grandma on her meals-on-wheels runs... Going up to the Olds College cafeteria to collect the meals. Their was some kind of soup broth in a styrofoam cup with a plastic lid that had a nick of a hole in the center. It smelled, not necessarily badly, and I think one time she might've let me taste some. But the thing that really drew my eye was the jello for desert, I think in a clear plastic cup with maybe a little dollup of canned whip cream on top. Of course we must've been driving around in golden brown LTD Crown Victoria, probably wasn't too old. Desert Tan Metallic. Of course grandpa would have a Canadian-built car.
